About Us

Haba was founded with the mission of connecting mama mbogas with customers.Our team came together to build a better world through E-commerce,we want to increase mama mboga sales with affordable price.

Haba is a platform that empowers communities to purchase fresh and healthy produce at lower prices by leveraging the power of group buying.Together, we can make wholesome food more affordable and accessible.
