FAQ

Download the app from Play Store. Use your mobile phone number and create a password to sign up for the application.

We do not provide delivery services, as the featured mama mboga vendors will be located in your vicinity.

Search for the Haba website on your browser and register as a vendor. Once registered, you can display your products and begin selling.

Any user can create a group and share the automatically generated link for others to join. To purchase a product as a group, the minimum quantity specified by the vendor must be met. Additionally, an individual can participate in more than two groups.

If a member leaves a group and the minimum quantity requirement is no longer met by the deadline, the group will be closed.

Each group remains active for a maximum duration of 48 hours.

Once a group meets the minimum quantity requirement, all participants will receive a notification confirming that the threshold has been met and that payment can now be made. Each member is responsible for paying for their own share of the products.

We also offer individual products, allowing customers to make solo purchases. One can buy group products alone required the minimum order quantity has been met.
